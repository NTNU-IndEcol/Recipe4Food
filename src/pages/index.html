Korean Bulgogi

Ingredients

  • 500g beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 garlic clove, minced

Instructions

  1. Mix the soy sauce, sesame oil, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Marinate the beef in the sauce for 20 minutes.
  3. Heat a pan and cook the beef until browned.

Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Total Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Calories: 200 kcal
